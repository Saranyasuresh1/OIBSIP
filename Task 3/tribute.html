Remembering Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Biography

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned Indian scientist and politician who played a key role in the development of India's civilian space program and military missile systems.

He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and rose from humble beginnings to become one of India's most respected figures.

Dr. Kalam served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was a dedicated educator, delivering inspiring speeches to students and emphasizing the importance of scientific research and innovation.

Quotes

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
"Don't take rest after your first victory because if you fail in second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck."
"You have to dream before your dreams can come true."

Dr. Kalam's legacy continues to inspire millions around the world.
